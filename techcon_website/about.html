About TechCon 2024

Discover the legacy, mission, and vision behind the world's premier technology conference

Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts in a university auditorium. What started as a passion project by a group of computer science graduates has evolved into one of the world's most influential technology conferences.

Over the past 14 years, TechCon has grown exponentially, attracting innovators, thought leaders, and technology professionals from around the globe. Our conference has been hosted in 8 different countries and has featured groundbreaking announcements from tech giants and startups alike.

Milestones include our first international conference in 2014, the introduction of the Startup Pavilion in 2017, and our first hybrid event in 2021 that connected over 50,000 virtual attendees with the physical conference.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to accelerate technological progress by creating spaces where innovation thrives. We bring together the brightest minds across industries to share knowledge, spark collaborations, and shape the future of technology.

We believe technology should be accessible, ethical, and transformative. TechCon serves as a platform to discuss not just what technology can do, but what it should do to create a better future for all.

Our core principles:

  • Foster innovation through open knowledge sharing
  • Bridge the gap between academia, industry, and startups
  • Promote diversity and inclusion in technology
  • Encourage ethical development and application of technology
  • Inspire the next generation of tech leaders

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential figures in technology over the past decade. Here are just a few of the visionaries who have graced our stages:

Dr. Elena Rodriguez

AI Ethics Researcher, Stanford University

Pioneered frameworks for ethical AI implementation. Her 2022 keynote on "Responsible Innovation" set new industry standards.

Marcus Johnson

CTO, QuantumLeap Technologies

Led the team that achieved quantum supremacy in 2021. His demonstrations at TechCon 2020 and 2023 drew record attendance.

Aisha Patel

Global Security Lead, TechFortify

Her 2019 presentation on emerging cyber threats predicted several major vulnerabilities that were later exploited worldwide.

Professor David Kim

Director, MIT Robotics Lab

Introduced breakthrough haptic technology at TechCon 2021 that revolutionized human-robot interaction.
